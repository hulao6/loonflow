# Project Roadmap

## 3.2.0 (Apr.–May 2026)

**MCP Server capabilities**

Expose standardized ticket APIs for external systems, supporting core operations such as ticket creation, handling, and status queries over the MCP protocol—laying the groundwork for external integrations and AI agent usage.

## Medium-term planning (end of 2026)

### 1. Experience and foundational capabilities

- **Full-text search upgrade** — Multi-criteria combined search, highlighted results, and quick filtering
- **Workflow engine enhancements** — Workflow categorization and visual orchestration
- **Finer-grained permissions** — Field-level and action-level access control
- **Ticket relationships** — Automatic sub-ticket creation and management of links between tickets
- **Form capabilities** — Additional field types (e.g. cascading selects and bundled widgets)

### 2. Intelligent data analytics

- **Ticket trend analysis** — Multi-dimensional aggregation and visual reports
- **Processing efficiency insights** — Identify bottlenecks; duration distribution and SLA attainment analysis
- **Custom dashboards** — Configurable monitoring panels for business teams

### 3. Deeper AI integration

- **Smart intake and routing** — Recommend ticket type, priority, and assignee from history and user descriptions
- **Assisted reply suggestions** — Standard answers and solution recommendations from the knowledge base for agents
- **Ticket summaries** — Auto-generated handling summaries and closure notes to reduce manual writing
